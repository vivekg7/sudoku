# Privacy Policy

**Sudoku - Offline & Ad-Free**

_Last updated: 15 March 2026_

## Overview

This app is designed to work entirely offline. It does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data whatsoever.

## Data Collection

**We collect nothing.** Specifically:

- No personal information
- No usage analytics or telemetry
- No crash reports
- No cookies or tracking identifiers
- No advertising data
- No location data

## Internet Access

The app does not require or use an internet connection. All features work fully offline.

## Camera Permission

The app requests camera access solely for scanning QR codes printed on exported puzzle PDFs. Camera data is processed locally on your device and is never stored, recorded, or transmitted.

## Local Storage

Game data (saved puzzles, bookmarks, statistics, and settings) is stored locally on your device. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to export it using the in-app export feature, which saves a file to a location you choose.

## Third-Party Services

The app uses no third-party services, SDKs, or APIs. There are no ads, no analytics platforms, and no external network calls of any kind.

## Children's Privacy

The app does not collect data from anyone, including children. It is safe for use by all ages.

## Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, the changes will be reflected in this document with an updated date. Since the app collects no data, meaningful changes are unlikely.

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can open an issue on the [GitHub repository](https://github.com/VivekYadavDeveloper/sudoku).

## Source Code

This app is open source under the GPLv3 license. You can verify all claims in this policy by reviewing the source code.
